Week 3‚Äì4: Insulation (and the $340 Mistake)

üì∏img36 : 9 landscape

Week 4: second attempt at insulation after the Amazon foam failure. Froth-Pak 650 only from here.

I bought a 2-part closed-cell spray foam kit from Amazon that advertised 200 board feet of coverage. Actual yield: 110 board feet ‚Äî and it didn't adhere properly to bare metal ribs. I had to scrape off everything and start over with a Froth-Pak 650 from a local supplier. That decision cost $340 extra and two weeks of time.

‚ö†Ô∏è Don't Buy Spray Foam from Amazon Always buy Froth-Pak from a proper HVAC supplier. Amazon kits are inconsistent on yield and adhesion. One bad kit costs more than the savings.

What I'd Do Differently

  1. Buy the right spray foam first ‚Äî Froth-Pak only. Saved $189 in waste immediately.
  2. Plan all electrical runs before walls close ‚Äî I had to drill through a finished wall for one forgotten cable.
  3. Get the 45L fridge not the 35L ‚Äî $60 more upfront, significantly more room every day.
  4. Add a diesel heater from day one ‚Äî Added a Webasto 3 months after move-in. Should have been in the original build.
