Lithium Cold Weather Warning

LiFePO4 batteries cannot be charged below 32¬∞F (0¬∞C) without damaging the cells. This is a hard limit. Solutions: buy a battery with an internal heater (Battle Born and Renogy offer this), insulate the battery compartment, or use a battery with a built-in low-temperature cutoff BMS. In the desert Southwest? Never an issue. Alaska in January? Plan accordingly.
