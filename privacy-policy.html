04 ‚Äî Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your browser. vanlife uses the following types of cookies:

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function. They do not collect personal information and cannot be disabled without affecting site functionality. We do not currently set any first-party essential cookies beyond standard session management.

Analytics Cookies (Google Analytics)

We use Google Analytics to collect aggregated, anonymized data about how visitors use vanlife. Google Analytics sets the following cookies:

  • _ga ‚Äî distinguishes users; expires after 2 years
  • _ga_[ID] ‚Äî persists session state; expires after 2 years
  • _gid ‚Äî distinguishes users; expires after 24 hours
  • _gat ‚Äî throttles request rate; expires after 1 minute

We have enabled IP anonymization in Google Analytics. This means Google Analytics shortens IP addresses within EU member states before any transmission to Google's servers. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Advertising Cookies (Google AdSense)

Google AdSense uses cookies to serve personalized advertisements based on your interests and browsing history across the web. See Section 05 for full details. The main cookies used include:

  • IDE ‚Äî used by Google DoubleClick to register and report user actions after viewing or clicking an advertisement; expires after 13 months
  • DSID ‚Äî stores user preferences for signed-in Google accounts; expires after 2 weeks
  • test_cookie ‚Äî checks if the user's browser supports cookies; expires after 1 day
  • __gads / __gpi ‚Äî stores ad interaction data; expires after 13 months

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies, delete existing cookies, or be notified when new cookies are set. Note that disabling cookies may affect site functionality and your ad experience. Instructions for managing cookies in common browsers:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Apple Safari
  • Microsoft Edge
