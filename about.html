The Origin Story

I BUILT MY FIRST VAN WRONG.
TWICE.

In 2021, I spent $14,000 on a Ford Transit build that had condensation running down the walls by November. I'd watched 60+ YouTube videos and read every blog I could find. Everything was either wrong, outdated, or quietly funded by the brands being recommended.

I tore it down to the metal and rebuilt it. That second build worked. VanWild is everything I wish had existed before build number one.

Today I've been living full-time in vans and (one brief skoolie experiment) for 4 years, covered 87,000 miles, and tested more gear than I care to admit. None of the brands mentioned on this site have paid me anything. If that ever changes, I'll say so clearly.
